Digital Signature Certificate Types

Choose the right DSC for your needs - from individual to organizational certificates

Individual DSC

Ideal for Indian citizens who want a digital signature certificate issued in their own name without linking to any organization.

  • Used for ITR, GST, MCA & EPFO submissions
  • Issued using Aadhaar OTP or biometric verification
  • Simple, paperless, and fast processing
  • Valid for 1-3 Years
Most Popular

Organisational DSC

Designed for employees or signatories of any organization who need a digital certificate issued under the organization's name.

  • Used in e-Tendering, e-Bidding, and legal documents
  • Requires proof of company authorization
  • Issued to authorized personnel of the entity
  • Valid for 1-3 Years

Foreign National DSC

Meant for foreign individuals and entities who require a valid digital signature for use in Indian legal or business processes.

  • Supports both individual and organizational DSCs
  • Requires passport, visa & address proof documents
  • Mandatory offline or video verification process
  • Valid for 1-3 Years

Document Signer

Perfect for businesses that need to digitally sign bulk documents such as invoices, reports, and official communications.

  • Streamlines automated bulk document signing
  • Integrates easily with ERP or backend systems
  • Issued in the name of the organization only
  • Valid for 1-3 Years
